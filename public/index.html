About The Tune Squad

Alex Cahn

My Recommended Mashup: Outkast & Glass Animals

Alex grew up right here in Seattle. His musical tastes have evolved over the years. Growing up, his family and friends influenced what he listened to. His big sister was listening to Soundgarden, Weezer, and Nirvana. His dad was a huge fan of Ray Charles, Stevie Ray Vaughan, and Seal. Mom was all about the Rolling Stones, the Who, and the Doors. His friends were listening to Eminem, Outkast, and anything by the Cash Money Millionaires. Nowadays, all the cool kids are into electronic stuff, and he's into it as long as the beats aren't too crazy. Alex was a cook prior to attending Code Fellows, and is happy and excited for this new challenge!

Allie Grampa

My Recommended Mashup: Billy Joel & Lady Gaga

Allie is originally from Cleveland, Ohio. By default, she is a Browns fan, which is a tough life to lead but she does it with passion. She started learning the basics of coding while working for a wedding website called Joy. Since moving to Seattle, she has spent a lot of time hiking and snowboarding. She is always on the hunt for the perfect sushi, perfect pizza, and perfect chocolate chip cookie.

Christina Lee
